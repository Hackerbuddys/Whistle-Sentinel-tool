    Welcome To The WhistleSentinel Product Tour.

    Delve into the intricacies of our system's features and capabilities, offering a detailed exploration of its functionality.

    Customised Reporting Site

    Easily customize text, branding (logo, color), and reporting form (add or remove questions for whistleblower, etc.)

    Issue-Specific Reporting

    Our system facilitates the reporting of a broad spectrum of misconduct, covering areas such as fraud, corruption, harassment, discrimination, safety violations, and unethical practices etc.

    Instant Alerts & Notifications

    Stay in the loop with our system's real-time alerts, providing immediate updates when a case is reported, assigned to an investigator, and when feedback is provided by the investigator.

    Fortified Communication Channels

    Our system incorporates cutting-edge encryption, multifactor authentication (2F), and robust security measures to uphold the integrity and privacy of every submission. Only designated personnel within your organization, investigators, and the whistleblower have access to the data. Even we, as administrators, cannot access your data or the incidents reported by whistleblowers, ensuring the utmost confidentiality.

    Efficient Case Management

    Our system enhances the investigation process through streamlined workflows, automated assignment of cases to investigators, and the setting of deadlines. This ensures a systematic and efficient handling of each case.

    Open And Transparent Workflow

    Empowering whistleblowers, our system allows them to monitor the progress of their reports, view feedback from investigators, and upload additional information and evidence. This transparency provides assurance that their concerns are treated with the utmost seriousness.

    Enhanced Case Linking

    Establish connections and link related cases within your whistleblowing system, providing a cohesive overview of misconduct or wrongdoing that share common elements.

    Dashboard Insights

    Within our whistleblowing system, the dashboard feature serves as a user-friendly interface, enabling stakeholders to effortlessly access crucial information and insights with just a glance.
